Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 9, 2026

Sun Digital Marketing ("we," "us," "our") respects your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect through sundigitalmarketing.com, how we use it, and the choices you have.

Information We Collect

  • Usage data: pages visited, time on site, referral source, and device/browser type, collected via Google Analytics 4 and Google Tag Manager.
  • Contact information: name, email, phone number, and any details you provide when you chat with Sol (our AI assistant), book a strategy call via Calendly, or email us directly.
  • Cookies: used by Google Analytics/Tag Manager to distinguish visitors and measure site performance. You can disable cookies in your browser at any time.

How We Use Information

  • To understand how visitors use our site and improve it
  • To respond to inquiries and schedule strategy calls
  • To provide the marketing services you engage us for

We do not sell your personal information.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third parties, each governed by its own privacy policy:

  • Google Analytics / Google Tag Manager — site analytics
  • Calendly — strategy call scheduling
  • Anthropic (Claude API) — powers Sol, our AI chat assistant
  • Netlify — website hosting

Data Retention

Chat conversations with Sol are not stored after your session ends. Contact form and booking information is retained only as long as needed to respond to your inquiry or provide services.

Your Choices

You may opt out of Google Analytics tracking using the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on, or disable cookies in your browser settings. To request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information, contact us below.

Children's Privacy

This site is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect information from them.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. The "Last updated" date above reflects the most recent revision.
